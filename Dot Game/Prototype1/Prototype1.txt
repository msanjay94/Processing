PROTOTYPE 1 - 14 jan 2015
	- 320x480 display(16:9)
	GOAL:
	    Given a particular direction, at constant speed, a ball is generated and it
	    moves along that direction at that speed, while at the same time decelerating
	    slowly.
	INPUT:
	    - Direction, from a static ball generator location(RED DOT), towards a moving
    	      position(GREEN DOT). Its position is changed by the mouse location.
